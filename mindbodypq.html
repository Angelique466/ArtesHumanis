What makes MINDBODYPQ different from regular coaching? +

Most coaching works with the mind alone. MINDBODYPQ works with the full intelligence of the body — addressing the somatic patterns and nervous system conditioning that regular coaching never reaches. This is why the results are deeper and longer-lasting.

Do sessions happen in person or virtually? +

All sessions are available virtually via Zoom. In-person sessions are available in the Brentwood, California area. The Immersive package includes an optional in-person retreat day.

How quickly will I notice results? +

Many clients report noticing shifts within the first 2–3 sessions. The depth and permanence of change grows progressively throughout the program as new patterns become integrated into daily life.

Is there a refund policy? +

Yes. We offer a full refund within 14 days of enrollment if you feel MINDBODYPQ is not the right fit. We are committed to your transformation and will always work with you to find the best path forward.

Can this be used for corporate teams? +

Yes — MINDBODYPQ principles are available in a corporate format as workshops and group programs. Contact us to design a custom engagement for your organization.
